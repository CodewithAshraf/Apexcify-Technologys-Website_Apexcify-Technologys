Security at Our Core

We prioritize the integrity and safety of your digital assets through enterprise-grade security protocols and continuous monitoring.

Infrastructure

Data Encryption

All data handled by Apexcify is encrypted at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.3). We ensure your proprietary information never travels over the web in plain text.

Development

Secure Coding

Our developers follow OWASP Top 10 guidelines to prevent common vulnerabilities like SQL injection, XSS, and broken authentication during the build process.

Monitoring

24/7 Surveillance

We utilize automated threat detection systems that monitor for unusual activity, ensuring immediate response to potential security incidents.

Access

Zero Trust Policy

Access to client environments is strictly controlled via multi-factor authentication (MFA) and the principle of least privilege.
